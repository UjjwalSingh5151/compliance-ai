Features

Everything your school needs

From scanning a single answer sheet to managing hundreds of students across classes.

📸
Scan from mobile
Teachers photograph answer sheets with their phone. The app stitches pages into a PDF and sends it to our AI for instant grading.
🧠
AI grading — question by question
Our AI reads the question paper and student answers, awards marks per question, and writes personalised feedback.
📊
Class analytics
See average scores, subject-wise performance, and identify students who need extra attention — across all tests.
📲
Share results instantly
Send results via WhatsApp link. Students and parents see the full evaluation — marks, strengths, and what to improve.
🏫
School management
Admin portal for schools: manage teachers, students, classes, and credits. Full oversight with role-based access.
📄
Question paper generation
Generate practice papers from any topic or syllabus. Export as PDF for printing or sharing digitally.
